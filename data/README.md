# Data

## Files

- **`BrentOilPrices.csv`** (not tracked in git) — daily Brent oil prices, May 20 1987 to Sep 30 2022.
  Download from the challenge's shared Drive folder and place it in this directory.
  Columns: `Date` (e.g. `20-May-87`), `Price` (USD per barrel).
- **`events.csv`** — 16 researched geopolitical, economic, OPEC, and sanctions events
  relevant to the oil market, compiled for Task 1. Columns: `event_date` (ISO format),
  `event_name`, `category` (Conflict | Economic | OPEC | Sanctions | Geopolitical),
  `description`.
